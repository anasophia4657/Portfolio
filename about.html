About Me!

Hi! My name is Ana Sophia, and I'm a junior designer. I grew up just
outside of Washington D.C. with my parents, who immigrated from
Portugal. Growing up and being surrounded by so many different
cultures sparked my curiosity and gave me a strong desire to explore.
Living in an urban environment also helped me develop a deep
appreciation for art and design. Once I realized I could actually pursue it,
I dove in and have not looked back since.


After finishing high school in the U.S., I had to face the big question that
everyone eventually does: Whats next? Thankfully, it wasnt as scary of a
question for me. I knew I wanted to be a creative, and I also felt a strong
pull to return to my home country. Going back to Portugal was important
because, although I experienced many cultures growing up in the U.S., I
wanted to reconnect with my own culture while still in the process of
discovering who I am.

That is how I found my way to IADE Creative University, right in the heart
of Lisbon. IADE is ranked in the top 50 design schools in Europe by the
Domus Ranking. The school gave me many real-world experiences in
design which im very grateful for! Thanks to these experiences and
lessons that are so applicable to the real world, I have never felt more
empowered in my education, and confident in my skills than I do now.
